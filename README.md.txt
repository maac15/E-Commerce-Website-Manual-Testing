
---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Validate **core business functionalities** of an e-commerce website.  
- Demonstrate **manual test case creation** for different modules.  
- Showcase **bug reporting & execution reports** in a structured way.  
- Provide a **portfolio-ready project** for QA interviews.

---

## 📝 Deliverables

- **Test Plan**: Scope, objectives, approach, and deliverables of testing.  
- **Test Cases**: Detailed test cases for Login, Product Search, Cart, and Checkout.  
- **Bug Reports**: Logged defects with severity, priority, and reproduction steps.  
- **Test Execution Reports**: Status of executed test cases and summary.  

---

## ⚙️ Test Environment

- **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox  
- **Test Data**: Dummy accounts and sample products  
- **OS**: Windows 10/11  

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Open the TestPlan folder** to view the complete test plan.  
2. **Check TestCases folder** to see module-wise test cases.  
3. **Review BugReports folder** for logged defects.  
4. **Open TestExecutionReports** to review execution status and summary.
